MindIE
Atlas inference engine, built on its hardware, streamlines model deployment with unified APIs and accelerated execution, debugging, and optimization, making app development faster, easier, and more efficient.
Superb Performance
Improves model inference throughput several fold, significantly reducing latency and inference costs.
Simplified Deployment
Supports simplified inference deployment on a single node or multiple nodes, as well as large-scale expert parallelism.
Light Load
Uses several optimization technologies, such as efficient memory management and KV cache compression, to reduce load.
Openness and Compatibility
Supports MindIE Service serving and multiple interfaces, including vLLM, OpenAl, and Triton.

Key Technologies

Integrated Serving Deployment
Easy-to-use serving capabilities and proprietary and open source solutions to facilitate service integration and deployment
  • Open MindIE Serving DeploymentMindIE serving supports multiple third-party APIs, such as Triton, OpenAl, TGI, and vLLM, for session management and request scheduling.
  • Open Source vLLM EcosystemvLLM is fully compatible with Atlas and supports its core features through open source vLLM-Atlas projects.

Use Cases

China Southern Airlines
China Southern Airlines uses Atlas MindIE to deploy DeepSeek, exploring innovative applications of AI computing power in flight operations, logistics, passenger services, and business management.
Longgang, Shenzhen
MindIE is used to access and deploy DeepSeek to enhance government and citizen platforms, facilitating the handling of government services and citizen inquiries.
Southeast University
MindIE enables local deployment of DeepSeek to integrate with the university's information service portal and app, serving teachers and students with capacity for 800 concurrent QAs and 100 million+ tokens daily.
